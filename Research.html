Turaev-Viro Invariants
During the summer of 2019 I was sponsored by the Stony Brook University Math Department's Math Summer Scholarship program.
I did research under the supervision of Oleg Viro.
We studied Turaev-Viro invariants, a certain class of topological invariants for 3-manifolds.
I defined my own (very simple) Turaev-Viro invariant.
